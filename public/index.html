Farming Techniques & Tips

🌾 Crop Rotation

Rotate crops annually to maintain soil fertility and reduce pest buildup. This ancient practice is key to sustainable farming.

💧 Irrigation Methods

Drip irrigation and sprinkler systems save water while improving yield. Choose based on your crops and climate.

🥗 Organic Farming

Use natural fertilizers and avoid chemical pesticides. Organic farming improves soil health and produces premium crops.

🌱 Seed Selection

Choose quality seeds adapted to your region. Good seeds lead to better germination and higher yields.

🧪 Soil Testing

Test soil regularly for nutrients, pH, and contamination. Proper soil management ensures healthy crops.

⏰ Timing

Plant at the right season for your crops. Proper timing maximizes yield and reduces disease risk.

Pest & Disease Management

🐛 Aphids

Symptoms: Curled leaves, sticky residue, yellowing

Solution: Spray neem oil or insecticidal soap. Use ladybugs for organic control.

Prevention: Plant garlic and chives nearby to repel

🦋 Caterpillars

Symptoms: Holes in leaves, frass (droppings)

Solution: Hand-pick caterpillars or use Bt (Bacillus thuringiensis)

Prevention: Use row covers on young plants

🍄 Powdery Mildew

Symptoms: White powder on leaves, curling

Solution: Spray sulfur or baking soda solution. Improve air circulation.

Prevention: Water at soil level, not foliage

🦗 Grasshoppers

Symptoms: Chewed leaf edges, skeletonized leaves

Solution: Use neem oil or spinosad. Install fencing.

Prevention: Remove weeds that harbor grasshoppers

🌱 Root Rot

Symptoms: Wilting despite wet soil, dark roots

Solution: Improve drainage. Remove affected plants.

Prevention: Use well-draining soil and proper watering

🦟 Whiteflies

Symptoms: Tiny white insects on leaf undersides, yellowing

Solution: Yellow sticky traps. Spray neem oil regularly.

Prevention: Plant beneficial flowers to attract predators
